Marvel Universes and Their Real-World Durations

  • Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) (Earth-199999)

    Duration: 2008 – Present (Ongoing)

    Description: The primary, interconnected universe of Marvel Studios' films and Disney+ series. It features the Avengers, Guardians of the Galaxy, and many other heroes. The main universe of the current phase of the MCU, called the Multiverse Saga, is focused on the dangers of the multiverse.

  • Sony's Spider-Man Universe (SSU) (Earth-68899)

    Duration: 2018 – Present (Ongoing)

    Description: Sony Pictures' universe focusing on Spider-Man-related characters, including Venom, Morbius, Madame Web and Kraven the Hunter. It exists alongside the MCU, with occasional crossovers facilitated by the multiverse.

  • 20th Century Fox X-Men Universe (Various Earths, including Earth-10005)

    Duration: 2000 – 2020

    Description: The universe of the X-Men, Deadpool, and Fantastic Four films produced by 20th Century Fox. It had its own distinct continuity, now being integrated into the MCU.

  • Netflix Marvel Universe (Earth-1218)

    Duration: 2015 – 2019

    Description: The street-level universe of the Netflix Marvel series, including Daredevil, Jessica Jones, Luke Cage, Iron Fist, The Defenders, and The Punisher. While initially having loose connections to the MCU, characters are now appearing in the main MCU.

  • Sam Raimi Spider-Man Universe (Earth-96283)

    Duration: 2002 – 2007 (with a brief revival in 2021)

    Description: The universe of the early 2000s Spider-Man trilogy starring Tobey Maguire. It's a self-contained universe with its own unique storyline.

  • "The Amazing Spider-Man" Universe (Earth-120703)

    Duration: 2012 – 2014 (with a brief revival in 2021)

    Description: The universe of the two "The Amazing Spider-Man" films starring Andrew Garfield. It's another distinct Spider-Man universe.

  • The Spider-Verse (Various Earths, primarily Earth-616, Earth-1610, and Earth-65)

    Duration: 2018 - Present (Ongoing)

    Description: This primarily animated universe, featured in "Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse" and "Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse", showcases various Spider-People from different dimensions. It has made some minor live action connections.

  • Marvel Animated Universe (Various Earths)

    Duration: 1992 - Present (Intermittent)

    Description: This encompasses a wide range of animated Marvel series, including "X-Men: The Animated Series," "Spider-Man: The Animated Series," and various others. Each series often exists in its own separate continuity. This is intermittent, as different animated series appear at different times.

  • What If...? Universe(s) (Various Earths)

    Duration: 2021 - Present (Ongoing)

    Description: The animated Disney+ series "What If...?" explores alternate realities where key moments in the MCU played out differently. Each episode typically represents a different universe within the multiverse.

  • Howard the Duck Universe (Earth-79101)

    Duration: 1986

    Description: The universe of the 1986 film "Howard the Duck." It's a standalone universe with a unique and quirky tone.
